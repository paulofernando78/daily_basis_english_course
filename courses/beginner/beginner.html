1A A Cappuccino, please?

Objective: By the end of this lesson, students will be better able to:

- introduce themselves using the verb to be with pronouns "I" and "You".

- count numbers from 0 to 10.

- describe the days of the week.

- say goodbye

Target language: Hi, I'm Helen. Hello, I'm Tom. Nice to meet you.

Grammar: verb be (singular): I and you

Vocabulary: numbers 0-10; days of the week, saying goodbye
